# Project Overview

## Project Name

AI Brand OS

## Tagline

The AI Operating System for Modern Brands.

---

# Mission

Build an AI-first SaaS platform that enables businesses to configure their brand once and let AI consistently execute marketing activities with minimal manual work.

---

# Vision

Become the operating system that powers every aspect of a company's AI-driven marketing.

The long-term vision includes:

- Brand Management
- Content Creation
- AI Characters
- Marketing Automation
- Customer Engagement
- AI Sales Assistance
- Continuous Learning

**Vision is NOT the MVP.**

---

# MVP Mission

Validate that businesses are willing to pay for an AI that permanently understands their brand and produces consistent marketing content.

The MVP is intentionally focused.

It is not a complete marketing platform.

It is the foundation of one.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Build one of the world's leading AI-native business operating systems for marketing.

---

# North Star Metric

Reduce the time businesses spend on marketing operations by at least **90%**.

---

# Product Philosophy

AI Brand OS is **NOT** an AI content generator.

AI Brand OS is **NOT** a social media scheduler.

AI Brand OS is an AI Operating System built around a persistent Brand Brain.

Every future capability grows from Brand Brain.

---

# Core Principles

- Customer Value First
- Validate Before Scaling
- Ship Small
- Learn Fast
- Simplicity Over Complexity
- Automation First
- Consistency Over Quantity
- Long-Term Scalability
- Modular Architecture
- AI Native

---

# Startup Principles

Every feature must answer:

- What customer problem does this solve?
- Is the customer willing to pay for it?
- Is it required for MVP?
- Can it be simplified?
- Does it reduce manual work?
- Can it wait until Version 2?

If the answer is unclear, the feature is postponed.

---

# Founder Principles

Always prioritize:

1. Customer Value
2. Validation
3. Speed
4. Focus
5. Simplicity

Never optimize for engineering over customer outcomes.
